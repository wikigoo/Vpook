## Overview

## Best Practices & Common Mistakes

**âœ… Best Practices:**

*   **Human Oversight is Central:** Treat AI as a powerful assistant, not a replacement for journalistic judgment. Every piece of AI-generated content must be vetted by a human editor.
*   **Create a Pronunciation Glossary:** Maintain a shared document with the correct diacritization for frequently used but hard-to-pronounce names, places, and technical terms.
*   **Master in Batches:** To save time and cost with tools like Auphonic, process several audio segments together as a batch production at the end of your session.

**âŒ Avoid These:**

*   **Trusting AI Facts:** Never take a factual claim from an AI assistant at face value. Always verify it against multiple primary sources.
*   **Skipping the Mastering Step:** Publishing unmastered audio with inconsistent volume levels is one of the quickest ways to sound unprofessional.
*   **Using a Generic Voice for Everything:** A serious political analysis should not use the same vocal tone as a human-interest story. Use the AI tool's settings to adjust tone, or use different voice models for different types of content.
